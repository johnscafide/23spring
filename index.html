About This Home

Split-level living,
reimagined.

Welcome to 23 Springlane Drive — a move-in ready 4-bedroom, 2-full-bath split-level in Blackwood, NJ (Gloucester Township). The main level features an open-concept kitchen with granite countertops, subway tile backsplash, and stainless steel appliances flowing into a bright dining area and living room with hardwood floors and a shiplap accent wall. The lower level offers a 4th bedroom, full bath, large family room, dedicated laundry, a bonus room perfect as a 5th bedroom or home office, and a rare 240 sq ft storage room. Outside: fully fenced vinyl backyard and an attached 1-car garage. No HOA. Minutes to I-295, Route 42, and Philadelphia.
