About This Home

Split-level living,
reimagined.

Welcome to 23 Springlane Drive — a move-in ready 4-bedroom, 2-bath split-level in Blackwood, NJ. The main level opens to a renovated kitchen with granite countertops, subway tile backsplash, and stainless steel appliances flowing into dining and a bright living room with hardwood floors. The lower level delivers a 4th bedroom, full bath, large family room, bonus room (5th bedroom, office, or gym), dedicated laundry, and a rare 240 sq ft storage room. Outside: fully fenced vinyl backyard and an attached 1-car garage. No HOA. Minutes to I-295, Route 42, and Philadelphia.
