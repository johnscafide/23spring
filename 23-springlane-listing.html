About This Home

Split-level living,
reimagined.

Welcome to 23 Springlane Drive — a spacious split-level in Blackwood offering 4 bedrooms, 2 full baths, and a layout built for real life. Main level features a bright living room, kitchen with island and pantry, dining room, and a spectacular 4-season sun room. Lower level delivers a 4th bedroom, full bath, large family room, bonus room (5th bedroom, gym, or office), dedicated laundry, and a rare 240 sf storage room. Fully fenced backyard with vinyl fence + 1-car attached garage. Minutes to I-295, Route 42, Philadelphia, and Gloucester Outlets.
